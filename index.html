THE CREATOR

I am Laura Garcon, a designer operating at the intersection of textiles, layouts, and interactive logic.

With deep roots in fashion design and graphic styling, I treat the digital canvas like an editorial scrapbook. Contrast is my playground: hard structures vs delicate drapes; rigid monospace grids vs fluid freehand illustrations.

Current Obsessions:

  • Heavyweight cotton drapes
  • Retro interface aesthetics
  • Tactile paper zines & print
  • Spun metal jewelry

THE DIALECTIC OF DRAPE

This ongoing research contrasts rigid tailorings (Structure) with fluid gather and flounce (Flou).

"We drape to find freedom, we structure to find boundaries."

By layering thick structured canvas jackets over sheer, featherlight silk chiffon skirts, we define a silhouette that shifts dramatically as the wearer moves in space.
