THE DIALECTIC OF DRAPE

This ongoing research contrasts rigid tailorings (Structure) with fluid gather and flounce (Flou).

"We drape to find freedom, we structure to find boundaries."

By layering thick structured canvas jackets over sheer, featherlight silk chiffon skirts, we define a silhouette that shifts dramatically as the wearer moves in space.
